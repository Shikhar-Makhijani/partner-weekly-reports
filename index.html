Recent Partner Challenges and Asks (refer broader tracker here)

Partner Demo Creativity & Tailoring — Feedback from the field suggests demos on partner + customer calls are not yet unlocking the "aha" reaction we need to convert conviction into pipeline — storylines often default to a standard walkthrough rather than being shaped around the partner's customer's specific use case (the Celanese SDA debrief this week flagged the same need for a sharper value prop). Investing in a shared demo repository and partner-tailored narratives (anchored on SDA and Search & Answers differentiation) will tighten the partner motion and improve hit-rate on joint calls.

Partner Caution / Slow Start — Partners continue to be conservative early on, testing the waters before opening their customer pipelines; demo quality on the first call is a direct lever to unlock this.
