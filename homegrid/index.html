HomeGrid - Trial Renewal Flow

Technical specification for WhatsApp bot renewal reminders and service cutoff

Overview

When a user's trial plan (2 weeks) is about to end, we need to send renewal reminders via the Onboarding Bot and handle service cutoff on the HomeGrid Bot.

Two Bots Involved

Bot Purpose
HomeGrid Bot The main bot where services are delivered (cook journey, household journey, day-to-day operations)
Onboarding Bot The bot that sends renewal reminder messages and collects Yes/No responses

Applies To

  • Cook Journey
  • Household Journey

Plan Details

Parameter Value
Trial Duration 2 weeks (14 days)
Renewal Price Rs. 249 / month
Post-Trial Plan Monthly (same renewal flow repeats every month)

Reminder Schedule

Reminders are sent via the Onboarding Bot starting 4 days before the trial/plan expires.

Day Days Before Expiry Messages Sent Notes
Day 11 4 days before 1 message First reminder
Day 12 3 days before 1 message
Day 13 2 days before 2 messages
Day 14 1 day before (last day of trial) 3 messages Services still active the entire day until 12:00 AM midnight
Day 15 Day of closure (trial expired) 3 messages Services are now OFF. Expired message on HomeGrid Bot.
Total reminder messages if user does not respond: 1 + 1 + 2 + 3 + 3 = 10 messages over 5 days.

Renewal Message (Onboarding Bot)

This is the WhatsApp message sent on the Onboarding Bot:

Hi! Your HomeGrid trial plan is ending soon. We hope you've been enjoying the experience!

Would you like to continue with HomeGrid services at just Rs. 249/month?
Yes, Continue No, Thanks

If User Clicks "Yes, Continue"

1

Send the payment link (Rs. 249) immediately on the Onboarding Bot.

2

Once payment is confirmed, activate the monthly plan. Services continue uninterrupted on the HomeGrid Bot.

3

Stop all further reminder messages. Flow is closed.

If User Clicks "No, Thanks"

1

Stop the entire reminder flow immediately. No more messages are sent on the Onboarding Bot.

2

Services continue on the HomeGrid Bot until the trial/plan period ends (midnight 12:00 AM on the expiry date).

3

After expiry, the expired auto-reply kicks in on the HomeGrid Bot (see below).

Service Cutoff Logic

Services are active for the entire last day of the trial/plan period until 12:00 AM midnight. At exactly 12:00 AM (start of the next day), services are cut off.

Timeline Example (2-week trial starting April 1)

Date What Happens
Apr 1 Trial starts
Apr 11 First reminder sent (4 days before expiry)
Apr 12 Second reminder (3 days before)
Apr 13 Two reminders sent (2 days before)
Apr 14 Three reminders sent. Services still active entire day. Cutoff at 12:00 AM midnight.
Apr 15 Services OFF. Three reminders sent. Expired auto-reply active on HomeGrid Bot.

After Expiry - HomeGrid Bot Behavior

Once the trial/plan has expired and the user has NOT renewed, every message they send on the HomeGrid Bot (both Cook and Household groups) gets this auto-reply:

Thank you for using HomeGrid! Your plan has expired. Please renew to continue using HomeGrid services.

This auto-reply stays active until the user renews via the payment link.

Monthly Renewal (Post-Trial)

After the user pays Rs. 249 and moves to a monthly plan, the exact same flow repeats every month:

  • 4 days before the monthly plan expires, reminders start on the Onboarding Bot
  • Same escalating schedule: 1, 1, 2, 3, 3 messages
  • Same Yes/No buttons, same payment link (Rs. 249)
  • Same cutoff logic at 12:00 AM midnight on expiry date
  • Same expired auto-reply on HomeGrid Bot if not renewed

Quick Reference - Complete Flow

1

User starts trial (Day 1). Services active on HomeGrid Bot.

2

Day 11 (4 days before expiry): First renewal reminder on Onboarding Bot.

3

Days 11-15: Escalating reminders (1, 1, 2, 3, 3) unless user responds.

4

User clicks "Yes" -> Payment link sent -> Payment confirmed -> Monthly plan activated -> Reminders stop.

5

User clicks "No" -> All reminders stop immediately. Services continue until expiry at 12:00 AM midnight.

6

No response / expired -> Services cut off at 12:00 AM midnight. HomeGrid Bot auto-replies with expired message.

7

Monthly plan: Same flow repeats every month, 4 days before plan expiry.

Stop conditions:
- User clicks "Yes" -> Stop reminders, send payment link
- User clicks "No" -> Stop reminders immediately, services continue until expiry
- User pays -> Activate plan, stop everything
